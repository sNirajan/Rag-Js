City Zoo - Ticketing & Entry Policy

Entry Windows:
- Standard entry windows are 30 minutes. Late arrivals may be accommodated at staff discretion based on capacity.

Rescheduling:
- One complimentary reschedule up to 24 hours before original entry time (subject to availability).
- Within 24 hours, rescheduling is not guaranteed and may incur a fee.

Refunds:
- Nonrefundable except for full-day closures initiated by the zoo.
- Partial-day weather holds may qualify for rain checks.

Identification:
- Photo ID may be requested for discounted tickets and memberships.

Re-entry:
- Same-day re-entry allowed with hand stamp and valid barcode.

Special Events:
- Separate tickets may be required for after-hours or limited-capacity events.
