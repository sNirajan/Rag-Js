City Zoo — Food & Retail

Outside Food:
- Outside food and non-alcoholic drinks are permitted.
- Glass containers, alcohol, and open flames are prohibited.

Picnic Areas:
- Picnic areas are located near the North Lawn and the Children’s Zone.

Concessions:
- Concessions include burgers, pizza, salads, and vegetarian options.
- Concessions operate daily from 10:30 AM; hours vary in off-peak seasons.

Retail:
- The gift shop is located near the exit. It sells toys, apparel, and eco-friendly souvenirs.
- Members receive 10% off all retail purchases.
