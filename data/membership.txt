City Zoo — Memberships

Benefits:
- Unlimited general admission for one year.
- Discounts on concessions and retail (10%).
- Early access to special events.

Guest Passes:
- Some tiers include guest passes. These are valid for general admission only.
- Blackout dates may apply during high-demand days (e.g., long weekends).

Renewals:
- Memberships can be renewed online or at Guest Services.
- Renewal reminders are sent 30 days before expiration.

Blackout Dates:
- Blackout dates are posted on the zoo’s website calendar each season.
