City Zoo — Visiting Guide

Map:
- A digital map is available on the website and at the main entrance via QR code.

Parking:
- Onsite parking is $10 per vehicle. The overflow lot opens on weekends and holidays.
- Four EV charging stations are available in Row C.

Accessibility:
- Most paths are paved and wheelchair accessible.
- Wheelchair rentals are available at the main gate.
- Family restrooms and nursing areas are located near the Children’s Zone.

Lockers:
- Day-use lockers are available near the main entrance.

Lost & Found:
- Visit Guest Services or call the number on your ticket confirmation.
