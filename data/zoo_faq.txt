City Zoo Visitor Guide

Hours:
- Open daily 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM. Last entry 4:30 PM.
- Summer hours (June–August): 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM, last entry 5:30 PM.

Tickets:
- Adult (13+): $25, Child (3–12): $15, Under 3: Free.
- Family Pass includes 2 adults + 2 children.
- Online tickets include a barcode for faster entry.

Food & Drinks:
- Outside food is allowed except glass containers and alcohol.
- Picnic areas available near the North Lawn.

Parking:
- Onsite parking is $10 per vehicle.
- Overflow lot opens on weekends and holidays.

Accessibility:
- Wheelchair rentals available at the main gate.
- Most paths are paved and accessible.

Weather Policy:
- The zoo remains open in light rain. Severe weather may cause temporary closures. Check alerts on the website.

Contact:
- Guest Services: 555-123-4567
